Overview

Speech sound disorders can affect a person's ability to communicate effectively. Software for intervention of speech sound disorders can help individuals with these disorders by providing targeted exercises and activities to improve their speech clarity and accuracy.

Benefits

  • Improved speech clarity and accuracy
  • Customized exercises and activities
  • Progress tracking and reporting
  • Accessible from anywhere with an internet connection

Features

  • Syllable segmentation and blending
  • Phoneme isolation and discrimination
  • Sound production practice and feedback
  • Articulation and phonological process goals
